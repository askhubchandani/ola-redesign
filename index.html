Better Forms

My attempt to improve the UX in forms.

Advantages: Know the person you are travelling with only after confirming your booking (security reasons) Know who’s being dropped first and in how much time How many co-passengers are you travelling with (Names not displayed unless you confirm your ride) Find the shortest route to your destination Show only co-passengers drop location in case the P1 dropped last or second.   Conclusion: Rather than sitting in car for hours for a 20min journey. It’s better to spend time in choosing from the options available. The information of Route and Passengers is already available. Just mapping to one destination is needed and considering passengers’ time should be the primary goal instead of distance from point to point.
